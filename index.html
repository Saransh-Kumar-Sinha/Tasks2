Welcome to Patna

Patna, the capital city of Bihar, is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world. Situated along the banks of the holy Ganges River, Patna has a rich history that dates back to over 2,500 years. The city was once known as Pataliputra and served as the seat of power for many great empires like the Maurya and Gupta dynasties.

Today, Patna is a vibrant city known for its educational institutions, political importance, and cultural heritage. Key attractions include Golghar, Patna Sahib Gurudwara, Bihar Museum, and the ancient ruins of Nalanda nearby. The city beautifully blends traditional charm with modern development.
